Maha Shivaratri 2017: Sudarsan Pattnaik pays tribute to Lord Shiva with sand art
Mumbai: Internationally acclaimed sand artist – Sudarsan Pattnaik – who hails from the beautiful state of Odisha, paid tribute to Lord Shiva on the auspicious occasion of Maha Shivaratri by creating sand sculptures at the Puri Beach and Bhubaneswar Airport.
